FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What are Resiligen PM skills for Claude?

Resiligen PM skills are PM-specific capabilities you add to Claude. Each skill teaches Claude a framework, methodology, or best practice — so when you run a command like /prd or /roadmap, you get structured, professional output instead of generic AI responses.

How do I use a Resiligen PM skill?

Download the skill file from Resiligen PM, add it to your .claude/skills/ folder, and you're done. Claude automatically discovers the skill. Type the slash command (like /prd) or describe what you need, and Claude uses the skill to help you.

Are Resiligen PM skills free?

Yes, all Resiligen PM skills are free to download and use. We believe every PM should have access to best-practice frameworks. The PM Operating System ($249) includes personalized context files for your company, all 60+ skills bundled, and agent team workflows.

What's the difference between Resiligen PM skills and ChatGPT prompts?

ChatGPT prompts are one-shot instructions that get lost. Resiligen PM skills are persistent capabilities — Claude remembers them across conversations. Skills also include frameworks and methodologies, not just formatting instructions.

How do Resiligen PM skills work with my company context?

When you load your company context into Resiligen PM, every skill automatically knows about your product, personas, and competitors. A /prd command generates a PRD for YOUR product, not a generic template.

Can I create my own Resiligen PM skills?

Yes! Skills are markdown files with a specific format. You can create custom skills for your team's workflows, proprietary frameworks, or company-specific processes. Check our docs for the skill authoring guide.
